About Us

This project focuses on raising awareness about protected wildlife species in Sabah, one of Malaysia's most biologically diverse regions. Sabah's natural environment is home to many rare animals such as the Bornean Elephant, Proboscis Monkey & many more. Unfortunately, human activities such as deforestation, poaching and land development have severely threatened the survival of these species. Many individuals, especially among the younger generation, are unaware of the importance of protecting wildlife & preserving natural ecosystems

Thus, this project hopes to serve as an educational platform designed to highlight the significance of Sabah's protected species & encourage collective participation in conservation efforts.

About Our Team

Our team consists of 10 students from the Foundation in Science Course.

Our Goals

Raise Awareness

To educate the public, especially younger generations, about endangered wildlife species in Sabah and the threats they face.

Promoting Conservation Values

To encourage responsible attitudes toward nature and highlight the importance of protecting biodiversity.

Strengthen Community Involvement

To inspire individuals and local communities to participate actively in wildlife conservation initiatives.

Preserve Sabah's Natural Heritage

To support long-term environmental sustainability by promoting the protection of ecosystems for future generations.
